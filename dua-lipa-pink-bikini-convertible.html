Dua Lipa Matches Her Sizzling Pink Bikini to Her Convertible

Publish date: 2024-06-24

Another day, another bikini photo dump from Dua Lipa.

This morning, the pop star shared a new array of bikini pictures to Instagram. Captured by photographer Tyrell Hampton, Lipa posed atop a cotton-candy pink convertible that happened to match her sparkling pink two-piece. Glittering pink sequins cover the swimsuit set, which also features gold chain straps that wrap around her torso and waist. She kept the monochromatic pink theme going with a matching sparkling mini handbag and a pair of metallic pink platform-heeled sandals.

As for the singer’s accessories, Lipa opted for chunky circular gold pendant earrings, stacks of gold bracelets on each arm, and a long pendant necklace.

She captioned the snaps, “~ suns out huns out ~”

The Grammy winner previously welcomed the start of bikini season with another summer photo dump posted to Instagram yesterday. At the time, Lipa wore a polka-dot two-piece from “La Vacanza,” the Versace collection she co-designed with Donatella Versace. The stringy black swimsuit features large white polka dots interspersed among a colorful print of bugs, featuring red and blue ladybugs and yellow and orange butterflies. She accessorized the look sparingly, with hoop earrings and a gold charm necklace.

Of her new Versace collection with the designer, which debuted in the French Riviera during the Cannes Film Festival last month, Lipa said in a press conference, “We wanted to make the holiday wardrobe and something that I’ll wear all summer, but also something other people will love and enjoy.”

Versace said in a statement, “Working with Dua on this collection has been very exciting and I love the dynamic between us. Dua is strong, fearless, and free and her creative vision is exceptional.”
