Tyler Gallo Obituary, Funeral Detuals, Tyler Died in Motorcycle Accident

Publish date: 2024-09-02

Police in Joliet report that a 20-year-old man was murdered on Friday night after crashing his motorcycle into a car.

Around 11 p.m., according to Joliet police, the guy was traveling north on Northeast Frontage Road close to Pandola Avenue when he collided with a vehicle that was going east on Pandola Avenue from south on Northeast Frontage Road.

The Will County Coroner’s Office reports that Tyler Gallo, 20, of Willow Springs, perished in the collision.

According to a press release from the Joliet Police Department, there were four occupants in the vehicle that Gallo struck.

According to the authorities, two female passengers were taken to a hospital. One woman was in danger of losing her life.

The traffic division of the police force is still looking into the collision.
