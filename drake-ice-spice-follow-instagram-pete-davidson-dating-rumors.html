Drake Re-Follows Ice Spice On Instagram After Pete Davidson Rumors

Publish date: 2024-09-30

Drake has re-followed Ice Spice on Instagram months after allegedly unfollowing the “Munch” rapper following an initial meet-up in Toronto.

Earlier this week, internet spies uncovered that Drake is once again following Ice Spice as rumors of a rift seem to have gone by the wayside.

Fans previously speculated that a bar on Her Loss was a shot at Ice Spice, when Drake raps: “She a 10 tryna rap, it’s good on mute” on “BackOutsideBoyz,” but The Bronx native clarified the two have spoken since then and there’s no beef.

“We’re cool,” the 23-year-old told The New York Times. “We spoke after that a couple times and we’re good. There’s no beef.”

Drake showed love to Ice Spice and co-signed her last summer, after which the 6 God eventually linked up with the “Gangsta Boo” rhymer in Toronto during 2022’s OVO Fest.

“When Drake DM’d me, I did not really expect that,” Spice said about the message she received from Drizzy. “I had just landed in L.A. for my Genius Open Mic for ‘Munch,’ and he was actually like, ‘Yo, ‘Munch’ is hard and your On the Radar freestyle is hard.’ And I was like, ‘Damn, that’s crazy because I’m really in L.A. about to shoot the Open Mic.’”
