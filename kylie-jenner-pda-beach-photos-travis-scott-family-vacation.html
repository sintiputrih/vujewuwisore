Kylie Jenner Shares PDA-Filled Beach Pics with Travis Scott

Publish date: 2024-09-23

Kylie Jenner and her family are enjoying a tropical vacation.

Yesterday, the beauty mogul shared some PDA-filled photos of herself and Travis Scott posing on a beach at sunset. In the romantic pictures, the two embrace while Jenner wraps her arms around her boyfriend's neck.

In another photo set, Jenner poses solo on the sand while wearing a white short-sleeved maxi dress with ruched detailing.

"Love like a sunset," she wrote in the caption.

On her Instagram Story, she offered fans a glimpse into their holiday by posting a video of herself, Scott, and their four-year-old daughter, Stormi Webster, observing lizards near the beach. In the clip, Jenner wears a backless bright green minidress as she stoops down to feed the reptiles, before asking Stormi if she wants to feed them too.

The couple also recently welcomed a second child, a baby boy, in March. Jenner opened up about her journey in an emotional video posted to YouTube.

"I just want to say to my postpartum moms that postpartum has not been easy. It's not been easy, it's very hard," she said of struggling through postpartum recovery. "This experience for me, personally, has been a little harder than with my daughter."

She added, "Once I realized that ... I was putting some pressure on myself, and I just keep reminding myself I made a whole human—a beautiful, healthy boy. And we have to stop putting pressure on ourselves to be back—not even physically, just mentally—after birth."
