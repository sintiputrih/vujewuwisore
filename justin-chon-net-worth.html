Justin Chon Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Justin Chon

$5 million

Justin Chon is an American actor. He had his career beginnings in the mid-2000s, debuting in an episode of Jack & Bobby. He next made his film debut in Puff, Puff, Pass. He later landed the role of Tony Lee in the sitcom Just Jordan, which was his first starring role on TV. At the same time, he played Eric Yorkie in the film Twilight. He reprised the role in the sequels The Twilight Saga: New Moon, The Twilight Saga: Eclipse, and The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 1. Also in the 2010s, he had notable roles on the small screen. He played Byron Summers in Sin City Saints, Seth Ko in Dramaworld, and Jordan Kwon in Deception. Chon also ventured into directing, making his directorial debut in 2015’s Man Up.
