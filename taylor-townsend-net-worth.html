Taylor Townsend Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-03-14

Taylor Townsend is an American professional tennis player who made her Grand Slam debut at Wimbledon in 2014, followed by the US Open, where she lost in the first round.

In the year 2015, she entered the top one hundred of the WTA rankings. In 2016, she reached the quarterfinals of the US Open in the doubles event. She has won multiple ITF singles and doubles titles.

Townsend won her first top-ten finish in 2019. In the second round of the US Open 2019, she overcame World No. 4 Simona Halep, a two-time Grand Slam champion.

To win, Townsend had to save match points against Halep. She eventually lost to eventual champion Bianca Andreescu in the fourth round, and her Round of 16 runs is her finest Grand Slam result to date.
