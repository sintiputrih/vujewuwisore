What movies has Michelle Buteau been in?

Publish date: 2024-02-18

What movies has Michelle Buteau been in? – Michelle Buteau is a talented American stand-up comedian, actress, television host, and host of Late Night Whenever! podcast. She was cast in the movies: Someone Great, Isn’t It Romantic, Sell By, and Always Be My Maybe, and in two television series: First Wives Club and Tales of the City in 2019.

  • Someone Great

Michelle Buteau played Cynthia in Someone Great, a movie that chronicles the experiences of a music journalist who recruits her two best friends for one last outrageous adventure in New York City before she moves to San Francisco after she was dumped by her long-term boyfriend.

  • Isn’t It Romantic

In Isn’t It Romantic, Buteau plays the 911 operator named Martina who answers Natalie’s call when she reports that, “Someone broke into my apartment, taken everything, and replaced it with much nicer stuff.”

Isn’t It Romantic is a 2019 American meta-romantic comedy film directed by Todd Strauss-Schulson and written by Erin Cardillo, Dana Fox, and Katie Silberman. The film stars Rebel Wilson as a woman who finds herself in a world where everything around her plays out like a clichéd PG-13 romantic comedy. Liam Hemsworth, Adam DeVine, and Priyanka Chopra appear in supporting roles.

  • Always Be My Maybe

Buteau plays Veronica in Always Be My Maybe, a 2019 American romantic comedy film, written by Ali Wong, Randall Park, and Michael Golamco and directed by Nahnatchka Khan.

It stars Park and Wong as childhood friends Marcus and Sasha, who have not been in touch since a brief teenage fling ended badly.

When Sasha returns to San Francisco to open a restaurant and romantic chemistry from their teenage years remains, Marcus’s fears and Sasha’s fame and demanding career challenge their potential new relationship.

James Saito, Vivian Bang, Daniel Dae Kim, and Keanu Reeves are among the other A-listers who starred in the movie.
