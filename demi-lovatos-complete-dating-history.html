Demi Lovato's Complete Dating History

Publish date: 2024-01-26

Demi Lovato has been through some ups and downs in her dating life but has learned to be careful when it comes to sharing her heart.

The singer’s most public relationships with Joe Jonas and Wilmer Valderrama both ended in heartbreak, but Lovato eventually picked up the pieces. She met Valderrama in 2010, shortly after her split from Jonas, and the pair quickly fell head over heels for one another. Even through her darkest moments, Valderrama was Lovato’s “rock” despite their age gap.

Lovato later moved on with actor Max Ehrich, who proposed in 2020. Us Weekly confirmed later that year that they called it quits.
