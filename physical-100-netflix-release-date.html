Physical: 100 Gets A Sneak Glimpse Of The Tudum Event On Netflix

Publish date: 2024-09-02

During the Korean leg of the TUDUM event, Netflix showed a new sneak peek of its upcoming Korean survival reality show Physical: 100.

On the show, 100 contestants compete against each other to see who has the best body.

In the sneak peek, you can see contestants doing hard challenges that will push their bodies to the limit. The competition will be shown live to people all over the world.

The way “Physical:100” is set up, with the voice echoing, reminds me of “Squid Game,” which was Netflix’s most-watched show in its first 28 days.

We don’t know when the show will come out just yet.
