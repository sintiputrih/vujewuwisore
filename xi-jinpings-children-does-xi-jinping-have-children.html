Xi Jinpings Children: Does Xi Jinping Have Children?

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Xi Mingze, nicknamed Xiao Muzi, is the only child of General Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Xi Jinping and singer Peng Liyuan.

Xi Jinping is a Chinese politician who served as general secretary of the Communist Party of China (CPC) and Chairman of the Central Military Commission (CMC) and has been the supreme leader of China, since 2012.

Xi Jinping has also been President of the People’s Republic of China (PRC) s ince 2013 and belongs to the fifth generation of Chinese leaders. The son of veteran Chinese communist Xi Zhongxun, Xi Jinping was exiled to rural Yanchuan county as a teenager after his father’s purge during the Cultural Revolution. He lived in a Yaodong in Liangjiahe village, Shaanxi province, where he joined the CCP after many failures and served as secretary of the local party committee.

After studying chemical engineering at Tsinghua University as an industrial-agricultural-military student, Xi Jinping rose through the political ranks in China’s coastal provinces. He served as Governor of Fujian Province from 1999 to 2002, before serving as Governor and Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Party Committee from 2002 to 2007. After Shanghai Party Secretary Tran Liangyu was fired, Xi Jinping was briefly appointed to replace him in 2007.

He then joined the Standing Committee of the Politburo (PSC) of the CPC the same year and served as First Secretary of the Central Secretariat in October 2007. In 2008, he was named the successor to the CPC Central Committee and is the supreme leader of Hu Jintao; To achieve this goal, Xi Jinping was appointed vice president of the PRC and vice chairman of the CMC. He officially received the title of core leader of the CCP in 2016.

Xi Jinping is the first general secretary of the CPC born after the founding of the People’s Republic of China. Since coming to power, Xi Jinping has introduced sweeping measures to enforce party discipline and impose internal unity. Who are Xi Jinping’s children?

Xi Mingze, nicknamed Xiao Muzi, is the only child of General Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Xi Jinping and singer Peng Liyuan.

Xi Mingze was born on June 25, 1992, at Fuzhou Mother and Children Hospital. She keeps a low profile and very little of her personal information has been revealed to the public. She studied French in high school, at Hangzhou Foreign Language School, from 2006 to 2008.

Xi Mingze enrolled at Harvard University in the United States in 2010, after a year of undergraduate study at Zhejiang University. She registered under a pseudonym and kept her identity private. In 2014, she earned a Bachelor’s degree in psychology from Harvard and is said to have returned to Beijing.

After the 2008 Sichuan earthquake, Xi Mingze volunteered as a relief worker for a week in Hanwang, Mianzhu. In 2013, she made her first public appearance with her parents in Liangjiahe village in Yan’an, Shaanxi, where they sent Lunar New Year greetings to the locals. She is described as enjoying reading and fashion.
