Wiki/Biography

Sarah Pidgeon was born on Sunday, July 7, 1996 (age 24 years; as of 2020), and she hails from Ann Arbor, Michigan. She did her schooling at Academy of the Sacred Heart, Michigan.

Career 

In 2018, she made her debut with the television series One Dollar in which she played a cameo role of a party girl. Thereafter, in 2019, she appeared in the television series ‘Gotham’ as Jane Cartwright. In 2020, she came into the limelight with Amazon Prime’s The Wilds in which she played the role of Leah Rilke. The series showcases the lives of a group of teen girls who must fight for survival while they are stuck on a deserted island after a plane crash.
