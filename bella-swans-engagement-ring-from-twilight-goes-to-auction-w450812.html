Bella Swans Engagement Ring from Twilight Goes to Auction

Publish date: 2024-07-06

You don’t know how long we’ve waited for this! If you’ve ever dreamed of owning Bella Swan’s heirloom engagement ring, now’s your chance. Props from the Twilight movie franchise, including the piece worn by Kristen Stewart in 2011’s Breaking Dawn: Part 1, will go to the highest bidder at Prop Store’s live auction in Hollywood on November 19 and 20.

Swan (played by Stewart) received the ring from her immortal love, Edward Cullen (Robert Pattinson) in the franchise’s fourth film. Per a Prop Store release, the bauble features 69 cubic zirconia diamonds in an oval pave and “14k RF” (meaning it’s 14 karat gold with a rhodium finish) engraved inside the band. Even though the diamonds aren’t real, they come with quite the sale price: The ring is estimated to fetch $3,000- $5,000.

The piece is set up in a satin black ring box with black plastic beads decorating the border along with a Prop Store Certificate of Authenticity. Bella’s wedding hair comb, her wedding bouquet and Edward’s stunt wedding band are up for grabs, too. 

In Eclipse, the third Twilight novel, Cullen offers Swan his mother’s ring to pop the question. “The face was a long oval, set with slanting rows of glittering round stones,” author Stephenie Meyer wrote. “The band was gold — delicate and narrow. The gold made a fragile web around the diamonds.”

Even though the final film was released in 2012, Stewart, 26, is still proud of her role in the wildly popular franchise to this day. “Anybody who wants to talk s–t about Twilight, I completely get it, but there’s something there that I’m endlessly, and to this day, f–king proud of,” she said in her February 2015 Interview cover story. “My memory of it felt — still feels — really good.”
