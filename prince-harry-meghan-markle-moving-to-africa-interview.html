Prince Harry, Meghan Markle, and Archie Could Move to Africa in Future

Publish date: 2024-06-20
  • Prince Harry has revealed that he's considering moving his family to Africa in the future.
  • In the new ITV documentary, Harry & Meghan: An African Journey, the Duke of Sussex explained, "our life's work will be predominantly focused on Africa, on conservation."
  • The news comes as royal sources reveal to BAZAAR.com that Harry and Meghan will be spending Thanksgiving in the United States.

Prince Harry is opening up about what the future holds for his family, and there could be some big changes ahead.

In a new documentary called Harry & Meghan: An African Journey, set to air on ITV in the United Kingdom, Prince Harry revealed (via ITV News), "I don't know where we could live in Africa at the moment. We've just come from Cape Town, that would be an amazing place for us to be able to base ourselves, of course it would."

However, the prince also reflected on the fact that it might be more useful for him to move around, and not just be based in one place. He said, "But with all the problems that are going on there I just don't see how we would be able to make as much difference as we want to without the issues and the judgement of how we would be with those surroundings."

Prince Harry did confirm, though, that he saw much of his future work taking place in Africa. As he told Tom Bradby, "I think it's a very hard place to live when you know what's going on but then you're sort of slightly disconnected from it. So, the rest of our lives, our life's work will be predominantly focused on Africa, on conservation."
