Publish date: 2024-06-17

Both Amell hunks are officially taken! Robbie Amell, the younger cousin of actor Stephen Amell, is engaged. The Flash actor announced he’s set to wed actress girlfriend Italia Ricci via Instagram on Wednesday, Aug. 20.

“She said yes!!!… I think. I can’t really be sure,” the 26-year-old captioned a sweet snapshot of the two kissing. “I’m going marry my best friend. @italiaricci.”
