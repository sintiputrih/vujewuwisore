Martin Lawrence Kids: See How Remarkable His Children Are

Publish date: 2024-10-03

The American comedian and actor Martin Lawrence has three children. He had them from this relationship with two of his ex-wives, Patricia Southall and Shamicka Gibbs.

In the 1990s, Martin Lawrence gained notoriety as a comedian by hosting HBO’s Def Comedy Jam, appearing in the Bad Boys series and Big Momma’s House, and starring in his sitcom, Martin.

Lawrence is the father of three adult daughters, including Jasmin Page, 28 years old, Iyanna Faith, 23 years old, and Amara Trinity, 21 years old.

Jasmin, the eldest child of Lawrence’s first marriage to Patricia Southall, the former Miss Virginia USA, was born. 

He and Shamicka Gibbs, a TV personality and entrepreneur whom he wed in 2010, have two daughters, Iyanna Lawrence and Amara Lawrence.

Martin Lawrence: Relationships and Family

In 1993, Martin Lawrence proposed to actress Lark Voorhies. In 1995, he married Patricia Southall, Miss Virginia USA. The couple’s daughter, Jasmine Page, was born on January 15, 1996.

After their divorce in 1997, Lawrence started dating Shamicka Gibbs.  Lawrence and Gibbs filed for divorce on April 25, 2012, claiming irreconcilable differences.

Martin Lawrence Children

Martin Lawrence is so lucky to be the father of three amazing daughters. Here are all of his daughters.

Jasmine Page Lawrence

Martin Lawrence and his then-wife Patricia Southall welcomed Jasmin Page Lawrence into the world on January 15, 1996. She studied at Duke University, where, in 2018, she received a degree with honors. 

“Congratulations to my firstborn,” her father wrote on Instagram to mark the achievement. You make me so happy, Jasmin. Keep going as you are. You have my utmost love.

Jasmin is currently a Los Angeles resident and pursuing an acting career in the same way as her father.

Her on-screen credits include Relatively Famous: Ranch Rules, Family Time, and Bad Boys for Life, a prequel to her father’s well-known movie.

Jasmine Lawrence and Eric Murphy

The relationship between Eric Murphy and Jasmine Lawrence is now public knowledge. They did not meet because of their well-known fathers, which is a humorous truth.

Jasmin claimed to InTouch Weekly that her uncle had made the introduction. Even though they’ve worked on two movies together, it wasn’t even our dad’s. They are close friends, she said.

Jasmin continued by saying that it is firmly established since their friendship was the beginning of their relationship. Through friendship, a romance developed.

She remembered, “And over time, it just, you know, became more.” According to Jasmin, their family is “absolutely” supportive of the situation.

Eric and Jasmin strive to follow in their dad’s footsteps while pursuing their goals. Jasmin is a performer who appeared in Martin’s film Bad Boys for Life, while Eric is a writer and voice actor.

Iyanna Faith Lawrence

Iyanna Faith Lawrence, the second kid, leads a reclusive existence despite occasionally appearing on her father’s and older sister’s social media profiles.

On November 9th, 2000, Iyanna Faith Lawrence was born. She was born in Los Angeles, California. She also identifies as African-American. 

In addition, her name means “God is gracious.” On July 10, 2010, her parents were married; however, they divorced on April 25, 2012.

She has successfully completed high school. Later, she applied to a college to further her education. She also enrolled at Sarah Lawrence College to get a bachelor’s degree.

Iyanna shares a close bond with her sisters, especially Jasmine. When she was leaving for college, Jasmine posted a heartfelt message that reads:

Iyanna, I’m such a proud big sister. You are beautiful, kind-hearted, passionate, resilient, tenacious, intelligent, and the list goes on. Sarah Lawrence College is so blessed to have you. Though my heart is heavy that you’re moving across the country, I’m excited for you because I know that God’s plans for you will surpass your expectations and wildest dreams. Keep being the beautiful being that you are. I love you, baby girl.

Amara Trinity

Amara Trinity, the youngest child born on August 20, 2002, graduated from high school in 2019. During her high school prom in May of that year, her mother, Shamicka Gibbs, expressed,

“Our beautiful daughter has transformed from a joyful little girl into a confident, mature woman.” According to reports, she wants to become a musician.

Martin Lawrence Arrests

Martin Lawrence was admitted to Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in July 1995 after he exploded violently while producing the movie A Thin Line Between Love and Hate.

He started acting erratically on May 8, 1996. He was eventually taken into custody after brandishing a gun in the middle of an intersection on Ventura Boulevard in Los Angeles and yelling, “They’re trying to kill me!”

He was admitted to the hospital again, with his publicist attributing this episode to tiredness and dehydration. He attempted to bring a pistol on a flight on July 29, 1996, and was sentenced to two years of probation and a fine.
