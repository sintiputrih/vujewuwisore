Biography

Jitendra Kumar was born in India on September 1, 1990. Indian actor who is best recognized for his comedy roles including on the sketch show The Viral Fever. He is known for his work on television and on several web series’ including the film Gone Kesh in 2018 and several TVF productions including TVF Pitchers in 2015. He starred alongside Shweta Tripathi in the 2019 feature film Gone Kesh. He earned his degree in Civil Engineering from IIT Kharagpur. He then went on to to attend the Hindo Technology Dramatics Society at IIT KGP. He was invited to star on TVF in 2012. His feature film debut came in 2014 when he starred as Laxman in Shuruaat Ka Interval.
