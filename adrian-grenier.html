Adrian Grenier News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Name:Adrian Grenier

Hometown:Brooklyn, NY

Relationship:Single

Birthday:July 10, 1976

About

Adrian Grenier has ascended to acting stardom with his role as Vincent Chase on HBO’s Entourage.

After getting his start in the 1999 teen flick Drive Me Crazy – at which time he reportedly dated costar Melissa Joan Hart – he began receiving critical acclaim for cult-favorites Harvard Man and The Adventures of Sebastian Cole. Since 2004, he has become one of the most recognizable faces on television for his role as the bright-eyed and bushy-tailed centerpiece of Entourage. His acting in HBO’s mega-hit comedy earned him a leading role in The Devil Wears Prada.

In addition to acting, Grenier directs and plays in a rock band called The Honey Brothers. Although gossip column rumors have been eager to pair him with the likes of Lindsay Lohan and Paris Hilton, Grenier has most recently (and most credibly) been linked to Australian actress Isabel Lucas.

The cast of Entourage are anticipating the release of their movie, which will be a continuation of the TV show, in June 2015.
