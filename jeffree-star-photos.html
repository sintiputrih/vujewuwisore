Photos Of The Makeup Artist Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-04-11

Jeffree Star poses at the Balmain Homme show, Autumn Winter 2017, at Paris Fashion Week Men’s in France in 2017. He showed off a multi-colored outfit and pink hair. 

Jeffree is best known as an internet personality and makeup artist who creates YouTube videos about his talents in the beauty industry. He is also the founder of Jeffree Star Cosmetics and started out using MySpace to promote his career. After building an extremely successful YouTube channel, he was named the fifth highest-paid YouTube star with earnings estimated at $18 million, according to ‘Forbes’.

In addition to YouTube and cosmetics, Jeffree is interested in music. He released his first studio album, ‘Beauty Killer’ in 2009 and it included songs such as ‘Lollipop Luxury’ which featured Nicki Minaj. He went on to tour to promote his music before quitting the music industry in 2013 due to legal issues Akon was facing from 2007 until 2010.
