Publish date: 2024-03-22

Was anyone cuter than Prince Harry as a young boy?!

Though Prince William’s younger brother is now 32 years old, royal fans are still fascinated with childhood photos of Harry because he was the most adorable, red-headed kid!

In a previous interview, Harry — who sadly lost his mother, Princess Diana, in August 1997 — shared a sweet memory of once visiting Disney World with Diana during his upbringing.

Harry during a royal outing. (Photo Credit: Getty Images)

“We went [on] Space Mountain 12 or 14 times, so much so that my [bodyguard] had to get off and vomit over the bench!” he said of the royal family’s 1994 trip to Disney.
