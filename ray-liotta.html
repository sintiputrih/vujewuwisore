Ray Liotta Biography

Ray Liotta (Raymond Allen Liotta) is an American actor, film producer, and voice actor. He is best known for his portrayal of Henry Hill in the crime drama Goodfellas (1990).

His other notable roles include Ray Sinclair in Jonathan Demme’s Something Wild (1986), Shoeless Joe Jackson in Field of Dreams (1989), Officer Pete Davis in Unlawful Entry (1992), Officer Gary Figgis in Cop Land (1997), Paul Krendler in Hannibal (2001) amongst many others.

    Ray Liotta Goodfellas

    Goodfellas is a 1990 crime film directed by Martin Scorsese.  The film is an adaptation of the 1985 non-fiction book Wiseguy by Nicholas Pileggi, who co-wrote the screenplay with Scorsese.

    It is based on the story of the rise and fall of mob associate Henry Hill and his friends and family from 1955 to 1980. Scorsese initially titled the film Wise Guy and postponed making it; later, he and Pileggi changed the title to Goodfellas.
