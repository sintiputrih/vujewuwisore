Johnny Depp and Amber Heard Are Married

Publish date: 2024-09-12

Update, 2/17: The first photos from Johnny Depp and Amber Heard's Bahamas wedding ceremony have surfaced. The bride wore a long white dress and flowing veil, while Depp donned a white tuxedo jacket. Heard's bridesmaids wore pale peach gowns, while Depp's son Jack stood by his side through the ceremony, Daily Mail reports.

Update, 2/9: After wedding in Los Angeles last week, People reports that Johnny Depp and Amber Heard hosted their official celebration this weekend at Depp's private island in the Bahamas. The small, intimate ceremony featured just two dozen seats (see the picture at People) which were set up on the beach in front of a white marquee. The reception took place on another part of the island, where a long table was set up for guests. Congratulations to the newlyweds!

Update, 2/5: Johnny Depp and Amber Heard are married, People reports. The couple tied the knot at Depp's home in LA yesterday and plan to celebrate with a bash on a private island in the Bahamas owned by Depp this weekend. 

Update, 2/4: Seemingly confirming Amber Heard and Johnny Depp's upcoming nuptials, slated to take place this weekend, a source close to Depp just revealed to E! News, "He's completely in love and can't wait." The couple is rumored to be saying "I do" at Depp's private Bahamas island over the weekend. 

Update, 1/30: Amber Heard and Johnny Depp are set to officially get married next week at his private island in the Bahamas, Page Six reports. After months of speculation, and a potential New Year's Eve nuptial ceremony, it looks like the couple will finally be tying the knot in a small ceremony. The intimate guest list reportedly will host 50 attendees including Heard's family and Depp's children—all of whom will stay on Depp's private yacht. The bride is rumored to be wearing a Stella McCartney wedding gown for the occasion. 

Update, 1/23: According to People, Johnny Depp was spotted wearing a new gold band at the premiere of his new film Mortdecai earlier this week, sparking speculation that he and fiancee Amber Heard secretly tied the knot. Their reps have not yet addressed the marriage rumors, but it looks like congratulations may be in order for the happy couple! 

Update, 12/19: After getting engaged in January, Johnny Depp and Amber Heard are reportedly planning to say "I do" in a surprise ceremony on New Year's Eve. Life & Style reports that multiple sources close to the actor have been invited to his private Caribbean island to ring in 2015 on Amber Beach—where the couple have been planning to get married for a year. Depp's closest friends, including Steven Tyler and Marilyn Manson, were invited along with both Johnny and Amber's families.

A source revealed to The Sun: "The big New Year party came as a surprise, he's invited most of his closest friends and their families to join him on his island. It could be a straightforward bash but friends have wondered if it could be a ruse to have a wedding without a big fuss." Only time will tell...

Original Post: 3/31: Johnny Depp confirmed his engagement to actress Amber Heard today. While promoting his latest film, Transcendence, in Beijing, the actor told the Associated Press, "The fact that I'm wearing a chick's ring on my finger is probably a dead giveaway. Not very subtle." A source told Us Weekly that Depp purchased the ring in question for Heard, but he's wearing it because it's too big for her finger. So how does Depp feel about wedding planning? "I think that I would be better at making women's shoes than I would be at wedding planning," he also told the AP. "I can't plan anything. I'm really bad at that stuff."
