Diane von Furstenberg's Beauty, Hair and Health Tips, Diane von Furstenberg Beauty Advice

Publish date: 2024-03-26

MAKEUP

"For me, it's all about smoky eyes and high cheekbones. Marlene Dietrich was the most fabulous woman ever. She is my icon." Daily routine: "I apply Janet Sartin makeup, Yves Saint Laurent Touche Éclat as concealer, and Nars's Multiple stick and Charlotte Tilbury's blush on my cheeks. I use brown eye shadows from Bobbi Brown, Charlotte Tilbury's mascara, and a nude gloss from M.A.C. I carry everything in my DVF Secret Agent clutch for touch-ups." Polish picks: "Every three weeks I have a manicure and pedicure at the office while I work on my computer. I keep my nails bare, but I like coral and pink polish on my toes."

Chris Coppola/Studio D

Nars The Multiple in Portofino, $39, sephora.com; Charlotte Tilbury Cheek to Chic Swish & Pop Blusher in Ecstasy, $40, charlottetilbury.com; Revlon ColorStay Gel Envy Longwear Nail Enamel in Royal Flush, $8, target.com; Diane von Furstenberg clutch (part of the Secret Agent tote), $498, dvf.com.

DIET & HEALTH

"I have a very healthy diet; I eat a lot of fruits and vegetables. On occasion I'll eat lamb, but I stick to more chicken and fish. I call myself a vegetarian, but then I would rather eat salami." Breakfast: A bowl of pomegranates with mango, papaya, or kiwi, and a cup of black coffee. Lunch & dinner: "I have my chef, Jane Coxwell, cook four to five little dishes, then I pick what I want." Favorite foods: A beet salad, couscous, curry, or soup. Sweet treat: "Côte d'Or dark chocolate is my weakness." Booze: Red wine or a shot of tequila. Weekly workouts: Yoga three times a week, and swimming or hiking in the country. Fitness gear: Eres bathing suits and Lowa hiking boots.

SKIN & BODY

"I've been seeing facialist Tracie Martyn religiously every Tuesday for 20 years. Her facials defy gravity—she keeps my face up." Skin essentials: Janet Sartin Cleansing Oil, Natura Bissé moisturizer, Tracie Martyn Enzyme Exfoliant and Firming Serum. Antiaging outlook: "I'm afraid of not recognizing myself [after plastic surgery or injectables], so I haven't done anything. So far, so good." Best bath buys: Kneipp salts and Elizabeth Arden cream. "I'm European; I Iove a bath."

Tracie Martyn Enzyme Exfoliant, $90, net-a-porter.com.  Natura Bissé Diamond Extreme, $345, naturabisse.com.

HAIR

"I've been coloring my hair with henna since I was 20 years old; Reyad Fritas [of Frédéric Fekkai] does it every three weeks. For trims, I see Alexandre Chouery at Frédéric Fekkai." Product picks: Moroccanoil shampoo, Bumble and Bumble Thickening Conditioner. D.I.Y. styling technique: "I blow-dry my hair, then roll it and pin it. Just before it completely dries, I remove the pins and comb it through."

Moroccanoil Hydrating Shampoo, $24, moraccanoil.com.
