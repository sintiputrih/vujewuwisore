Dolores Dorn

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Birth Name: Dolores M. Heft

Date of Birth: March 3, 1934

Place of Birth: Chicago, Illinois, U.S.

Date of Death: October 5, 2019

Ethnicity:
*father – German, Czech Bohemian
*mother – Lithuanian, likely Eastern Slavic

Dolores Dorn was an American film and television actress. She was known for starring in the films The Bounty Hunter, Uncle Vanya, and Underworld U.S.A. She was billed as Dolores Dorn-Heft on Broadway.

She was the daughter of Alice Ellen and Edward Heft.

Dolores’ paternal grandfather was named Edward Heft. Edward was born in Illinois, to German parents.

Dolores’ paternal grandmother was named Antonette Barcal (the daughter of Steve Barcal). Antonette was born in Chicago, to parents from Bohemia, then in the Austro-Hungarian Empire.

Dolores’ maternal grandfather was named Stanley Yagmin/Iagmin/Eagmin. The surname Yagmin appears frequently in Russia, Ukraine, and Belarus. Stanley was an emigrant, from Lithuania, then in the Russian Empire.

Dolores’ maternal grandmother was named Petronella Marma. Petronella was an emigrant from Lithuania.
