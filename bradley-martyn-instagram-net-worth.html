Who is Bradley Martyn and what is his net worth?

Publish date: 2024-01-18

YOUTUBER and fitness coach Bradley Martyn is a fitness influencer and self proclaimed gym rat.

The influencer has almost five million followers on Instagram as of March 2023, but who is he and how did he become famous?

Who is Bradley Martyn?

Born on May 22, 1989, Bradley is an online fitness influencer and coach from California.

He began his own training program called BMFit in 2006, and later dropped out of college to pursue his fitness dreams.

For a while he took part in amateur bodybuilding competitions but did not get into the professional circuit.

In a 2017 interview, he revealed the real reason he quit bodybuilding was due to the fact that the sport had become about social media numbers rather than the sport itself.

In 2014, his account took off, and he's gone into various business ventures since.

He founded AlphaCre8tivedesigns in 2012, and also opened Zoo Culture Gym in Los Angeles.

He's also ventured into supplements, opening his own line called Origin Supplements.

As well as a huge following on Instagram, Bradley has 4.1 million fans on TikTok, where he regularly has over one million views on his videos.

He has three million subscribers on his YouTube channel and he fronts a podcast called Raw Talk.

What is Bradley Martyn's net worth?

Bradley is estimated to be worth about $2million as of 2023.

Through his various business ventures and even a few TV appearances, he's built a comfortable savings account.

He appeared in the TV series Nelk Boys in 2022, as well as Coded Court in 2018 and Caught.

Is Bradley Martyn married?

As of 2023, Bradley is not married, and appears to be single.

He also has no children.
