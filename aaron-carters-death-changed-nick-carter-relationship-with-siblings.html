Did Aaron Carter's Death Tear Nick Carter Further Away From His Siblings Or Bring Them Closer Togeth

  • The passing of Aaron Carter has been incredibly difficult for Nick and his family.

Before Aaron Carter's awful death in 2022, he and Nick Carter had a tough relationship. While they both entered the music industry at young ages, their lives went in different directions. Nick joined the Backstreet Boys and Aaron faced years of suffering and pain. Even though Aaron was a popular singer in his own right, all the cash and celebrity in the world can't help someone feel better.

Now that Aaron is gone, his family is of course still affected by that terrible day when they learned that he had passed away. In some cases, tragedy will bond people and they will let go of any problems they had in the past. In other cases, things are still as complicated as ever. Nick Carter has two siblings who are still alive: Aaron's twin sister Angel Carter, and Bobbie Jean Carter. He also has three half-siblings: Kaden Brent Carter, Taelyn Dobson, and Virginia Marie Carter. Here's whether Nick Carter has a relationship with his siblings today.

Does Nick Carter Get Along With His Sister Bobbie Jean Carter?

There aren't any details available about Nick Carter's half-siblings Virginia Marie Carter, Taelyn Dobson, and Kaden Brent Carter. According to The Sun, Kaden is 17 years old, but that seems to be the only thing people are aware of.

Nick Carter's sister Bobbie Jean Carter, however, is a bit more of a public figure. Unfortunately, though, Bobbie Jean Carter has gotten into legal trouble, and that is why people know who she is.

That wasn't the first time that Bobby Jean was in the news. Radar Online reported that back in 2002, she was arrested for allegedly hurting two people back in 2002. The publication reported that Bobbie Jean was with Angel at the time.

The police said that Bobbie Jean was "sticking out the back window up to her waist wanting to exit the vehicle to talk. She was upset saying that three girls had jumped her sister and she protected her. She continued by saying that this is what happens when you mess with her family, adding that they are a close family."

At the time, Bobbie Jean was 20, and now she's 41. There is no evidence that she and Nick Carter see each other or seem to even be part of each other's lives these days. Fans do know that Nick spent some time distancing himself from Aaron Carter when Aaron was going through a lot. Sadly, the brothers weren't good on terms when Aaron died.

Nick has been honest about feeling bad about his family, though. It never seems like this is something that he takes lightly, and he seems to think about his siblings all the time.

Nick said, "I understand exactly what you're going through when it comes to having a hard upbringing in a family. No matter what happens, or what they say or do, you still love them."

Does Nick Carter Get Along With His Sister Angel Carter?

While it doesn't look like Nick Carter has said that he and Angel Carter get along really well, they have come together in the wake of Aaron Carter's death. It's possible to assume that they love each other and that they are supporting each other through this terrible time.

In November 2022, The Daily Mail reported that the brother and sister were at a Christmas concert that took place at Los Angeles's The Grove. Angel and Nick were seen chatting with Lance Bass and seemed glad to be there.
