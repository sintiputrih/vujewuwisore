Olga Fonda Bio, Age, Husband, Career, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-03-06

Olga Fonda Biography

Olga Fonda is a Russian-American film and television actress and model. She has appeared in minor roles on TV series such as Nip/Tuck, Ugly Betty, Entourage, How I Met Your Mother, Melrose Place, and Svetlana. She is currently starring in the fifth season of “The Vampire Diaries” as Nadia. She has been active in the acting industry since 2005. Her dream of being a model came true when she was on a vacation in Los Angeles.

Olga Fonda Age

She was born on October 1, 1982, in Ukhta, Russia.

Olga Fonda Husband

She was married to Michael Fundaminski in 2000. Unfortunately, they separated in 2008. In 2011 she started dating Liam White, a former actor who is now in the hotel business. The couple later engaged in January 2014. Unfortunately, the couple has not yet released details about their wedding day and preparations.

Olga Fonda Career

At age 14, she left her hometown to be an exchange student in the United States. After returning home, she continued her education by studying Business-Economics at a university in Moscow. She began modeling while vacationing in Los Angeles, California. She was quickly signed by Ford Models when they recognized her star-potential.
After acting in TV commercials, she played a Russian ballerina in Love Hurts.
Since 2007 she has modeled in different countries, for instance, Japan, Italy, and the United States. She went on to appear in television series including How I Met Your Mother, Nip/Tuck, Melrose Place, and Entourage, and played Owen Wilson’s girlfriend in a nonspeaking role in Little Fockers. She was cast in 2011 Crazy, Stupid, Love, though her scene did not make the final cut; she does appear in the film’s trailer.

In 2010, she was cast in The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn. She had a supporting role in Real Steel in 2011, her participation in Real steal played a major role in making her famous. Additionally. she played Nadia in The Vampire Diaries.
She is best known for her recurring role in Agent X opposite Sharon Stone and Jeff Hephner.
