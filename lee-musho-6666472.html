Lee Musho - InStyle

Publish date: 2024-03-12

Education: Syracuse University

Lee is a contributor to InStyle where she interviews top stylists and fashion experts to determine which brands are worth buying.

Highlights:

  • Lee has written for InStyle since June 2022, covering all things fashion
  • She has a B.A. in Magazine Journalism from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University
  • Over the years her work has appeared in digital and print publications such as Travel & Leisure, Food & Wine, Real Simple, and more

Experience

Lee began her career as a fashion stylist in New York City, assisting with editorial photo shoots. From there, she worked as an assistant producer for Bon Appetit and interned for the Cut. Now, Lee is a branded content editor for Vox Media and a contributing writer to InStyle.

Education
