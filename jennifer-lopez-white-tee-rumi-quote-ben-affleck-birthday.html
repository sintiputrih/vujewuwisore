Jennifer Lopez Wore a White Tee With a Romantic Quote on Ben Affleck's Birthday

Publish date: 2024-03-14

Jennifer Lopez subtly shared her love for husband Ben Affleck via her latest athleisure look.

The singer was seen out and about in Beverly Hills on Wednesday, the day after Affleck’s 51st birthday, wearing a semi-sheer white tee printed with a romantic quote from the 13th-century poet Rumi. The message read, “You are the soul of the universe, and your name is love,” and was punctuated with a small multicolored heart.

She paired the romantic top with classic black leggings and colorful accessories, including a pair of Nike sneakers with orange, blue, and green detailing, and a multicolored tote with gold hardware. Her hair was pulled back in a bun, and she completed the look with oversize black sunglasses, a gold necklace with a heart pendant, and a nude lip.

Earlier that day, Lopez also marked her husband's special day by sharing a candid video of them together. In the clip, shared on Instagram, the stars are seen sitting in their car, driving while singing along to “Wonderful World,” by Sam Cooke. J.Lo smiles as she films the sweet moment, while Affleck seems unaware of the camera as he sings the classic love song.

“Dear Ben … Happy Birthday. I love you!” she captioned the post.

The celeb couple have enjoyed some private time with their blended family since their most-recent red carpet appearance last June, when they attended the Los Angeles premiere of The Flash in coordinating black ensembles. In July, they celebrated J.Lo’s birthday—and looked so in love in photos the singer shared from the celebrations. And earlier this month, the two were seen enjoying a summer holiday in the Hamptons, New York, where Affleck turned heads in a graphic tee from Dunkin’ Donuts.
