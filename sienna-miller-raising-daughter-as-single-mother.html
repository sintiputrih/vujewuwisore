Sienna Miller on co-parenting with ex Tom Sturridge: 'we do bedtime every day'

Publish date: 2024-03-08

    Although her relationship with Tom Sturridge ended almost two years ago, Sienna Miller admits that her ex is still close by. "We do bedtime every day," the actress said inside of Allure magazine's May issue. "We felt like as much togetherness as possible would be ideal, and fortunately we really love each other and are best friends, and so that works. It's not that it's not complicated, because it is." The  actress and the English actor, who are parents to four-year-old Marlowe, ended their relationship in the summer of 2015.

     Sienna opens up about being a single mother Photo: Getty Images 

    Since then, the 35-year-old has embraced being a single mother. "I had an amazing moment the other day where I just heard this 'Mama!' from upstairs," she expalined. "I said, 'I'm coming, I'm coming.’ And as I got to the landing I just smelled, like, puke. And she'd thrown up basically off the top bunk, so the splatters were like: Pow! Like all four walls. She had the norovirus or whatever. I was like, 'I'm coming, I'm coming!' And I skidded on the sick and fell. Whacked my head. Then I get her out of the bunk; she's crying, covered in sick. I take her to the bathroom, take all her clothes off, and then the dog comes up and starts eating the sick. And I get her in the bath and in my bed, and I'm just, like, literally naked, mopping, and crying at midnight."

    Sienna and Tom are parents to four-year-old Marlowe Photo: Getty Images 

    Despite the not so glamorous parts of motherhood, Sienna wouldn't change anything for the world. She continued: "You know, and that's parenthood. You're so enriched by it and so fulfilled." The challenges she faces as a mother now have not discouraged her from having more children. When asked if she has plans on expanding her family, the actress responded with laughter, "I would love to. Yeah. I have to figure out the other side of it." Although more children aren't in the immediate future, the single actress, who recently refuted any romance rumours involving Brad Pitt, has a specific vison for her life years down the road. "I see a big lunch table. Outside. It's filled with food. And I want to be that wise, happy granny. And I'm the matriarch at this palazzo in Tuscany, and I'm cooking and looking after little babies."
