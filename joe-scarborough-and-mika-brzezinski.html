Josie Bates and Kelton Balka

The Bringing Up Bates stars tied the knot in an outdoor ceremony overlooking the Great Smoky Mountains at Cove Lake State Park in Caryville, Tennessee, on October 5. Josie’s family members, including Alyssa Webster, Erin Paine, Tori, Lawson, Whitney, Trace, Katie and Carlin Bates were included in the wedding, as were Kelton’s siblings Ariana and Grant. Several members of the Duggar family also in attendance. The bride, who wore a custom gown by Project Runway season 5 winner Leanne Marshall, was escorted down the aisle by her father Gil, who also officiated the ceremony. Following the nuptials, guests played volleyball and tennis and enjoyed a wedding cake made by Josie’s Aunt Kay.
