Van Vicker Reacts To Another Dead Video Of His Says He Has Never Been Involved In An Accident

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Actor Van Vicker has reacted to another dead video of his that is supposed to have happened after he was involved in a ghastly accident saying he has never been involved in an accident.

The video is supposed to sympathize with friends and loved ones over the demise of Van Vicker claiming his smiles will be missed and he has reacted to that saying he has never been involved in any accident as the video circulating claims.

It’s obvious some insensitive people that wish to see Van Vicker dead are the ones circulating such video and he has urged all his family, friends, and loved ones to ignore it because that is not true and only lies perpetuated by some insensitive people.

According to Van Vicker, this isn’t the first time such news is circulating online and we wonder why someone will just claim another is dead when the person is alive, moreover, you don’t even have access to the person in any way.

I would habitually overlook yet another ‘death video’ of me made by insensitive people; however, this particular one is making sensational circulation hence my post.

I am well by HIS GRACE. I have not been involved in any accident, neither am I dead. Perish the thought.

There has to be a way to disallow such lunacies, faux news, sensationalism and or impassivity via social media.
