How do I cancel my winc membership?

You can ask the Winc customer support to cancel your membership by dialing 855-282-5829. Make sure to request a confirmation email once they cancel the subscription.

Is winc easy to cancel?

There are two easy options to cancel your Winc account. You can live chat with one of the Winc concierges via the support button on their website. Alternatively, if you prefer, you can call (855) 282-5829 and speak directly to one of the Winc concierges.

Does winc give refunds?

You may cancel your Winc Branded Membership any time. If you cancel at least 3 days prior to the shipment of your initial order, you will receive a full refund of the Winc Branded Membership purchase price.

Is winc a subscription?

Winc’s membership itself is free. But each month Winc will send you at least three bottles coming in at $39, with flat $9 shipping fee. But Winc offers shipping on the house with orders of four or more bottles, so that’s the better deal.

How much does winc cost per month?

How Much Does WINC Cost a Month? The cheapest WINC membership option starts at $39 per month for three $13 bottles of wine. The most expensive wine they offer is $32, which would be $96 per month. Shipping is $9 per month for 3 bottles of wine.

Can you cancel winc at any time?

Yes, you can cancel and there is no cancellation fee.

Can you skip a month on winc?

The Skip a Month feature is unlimited. You can use it as often as you like, free of charge. To skip your monthly order, please sign in and click the Skip a Month button under the My Membership section. Be sure to do this 48 hours prior to your order date to ensure that your order does not ship.
