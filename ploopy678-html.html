Ploopy678 - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-10-07

Chinese content creator who became a public figure after starting her Ploopy678 YouTube channel. She posts a mixture of dancing and choreography clips that often feature K-pop music for over 320,000 subscribers.

Before Fame

She began studying to be a pharmacist at University College London.

Trivia

Her first YouTube video to surpass 2 million views was 2019's Evolution of KPOP Dance (Iconic KPOP Dances Through the Years).

Family Life

She moved from Hong Kong to the United Kingdom. Her real name is Evangeline Pang.

Associated With

She shared dance footage set to Ariana Grande's "7 Rings" in February 2019.
