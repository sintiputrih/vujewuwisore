From Sex Education Fame to Red Rose Thrills Acting Journey, Personal Life, and Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-24

You might have recognized Ashna Rabheru as Gwen in "Sex Education," but did you know she started acting at 17? She graduated from the Royal Welsh College of Music & Drama and began her acting career not long ago. 

Meet Ashna, the well-known English actress from the Netflix show "Sex Education." Get to know more about Ashna's acting journey, personal life, social media, and net worth in this article.

Get To Know Ashna Rabheru

Ashna is a talented actress, and as a fun fact, she also is a writer. As of 2024, she is 27 years old. Born on December 30, 1996, in Basingstoke, England, Ashna studied at a community school called Brighton Hill. She then went on to study at Queen Mary's College. From a young age, Rabheru was fond of films and acting.

Ashna studied acting at The Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama, graduating with a BA Honours in Acting in 2018. Before that, she attended the Identity Drama School from 2014 to 2015, and from 2011 to 2014, she was at The Hunt Academy (HAYA).

At the age of 17, Ashna was signed to Identity Agency Group. She then makes her debut on the big screen as an actor in the TV series, "Indian Summer." Soon, Rabheru's career in acting took off. 

Ashna Rabheru's Acting Journey

Ashna discovered she was going to be an actress early on. Following her debut at 17, she appears in many other movies and TV shows. Right after starring in "Indian Summer," Ashna starred as Gewn in the 2019 Netflix original series, "Sex Education."

Ashna isn't just a movie and TV star – she's also been a tutor and acting teacher. At HAYA, a British agency for budding actors, she worked as both an acting tutor and Audition Specialist, helping young talents break into the industry.

Currently, Ashna is working on the new Netflix horror series, "Red Rose." It is a teenage horror series in which Ashna plays the role of Jaya Mahajan, one of the main characters in the show. People love the show and Ashna's character.

Ashna Rabheru in "RED ROSE" Series

Ashna, after finishing acting in the series, "Sex Education," went on to star in the new horror series, "Red Rose." The horror TV series "Red Rose" first aired in August 2022 and was later adopted by Netflix in 2023. She starred alongside another actress, Silvie Furneaux.

In the series, Rabheru takes on the role of Jaya Mahajan. The storyline is set in Bolton and revolves around a group of friends grappling with a mysterious new app. In the show, Rabheru is outcasted by her friends, but despite being an outcast, she plays a crucial role in revealing the app's mysteries. 

Is Ashna Rabheru Dating Anyone?

Ashna is a dedicated and up-and-coming celebrity who gained fame early on while maintaining a low-key public image. Fans are curious about her romantic life, but as of now, she is single and not in a relationship. 

Rabheru keeps her past and present relationships private, choosing to prioritize her career and education at the moment. However, it may be that she is secretly dating someone, away from the eyes of the public. Whatever the case, we will surely update you on her relationship status.

Ashna Rabheru on the Radio.

Ashna hasn't confined herself solely to the screen with her acting; she has also voiced on numerous radio channels in the UK. She appeared on BBC Radio 4 and BBC Radio Wales, the two most prominent radio channels. 

Among Ashna's radio credits are notable works such as "The System" on BBC Radio 4, "Electric Decade: The Good Soldier," "Bottled," "Deacon: Moonlight on Water," "Splott," "Dangerous Visions: Perimeter," and "Where This Service Will Continue."

Is Ashna Rabheru Active on Social Media?

As an actor, Ashna is active on Twitter, having joined in November 2009 and amassing a following of over 2,000. She occasionally posts and promotes her movies and TV shows featuring her. In her recent post on Twitter, Ashna reposted a tweet from Disney about the release of the show, "Renegade Nell."

Aside from Twitter, Ashna also has an Instagram and a Facebook account. However, she has not posted much on her other social platforms and is inactive on them. On Instagram, she has only posted once, featuring the trailer of the TV series "Red Rose" in which she played a starring role.

Ashna Rabheru Net Worth

Ashna Rabheru, a rising talent in entertainment, is set for more financial success with an estimated net worth of around $500,000, similar to the net worth of another actress, Nicole Carpenter.

Through acting and writing in various projects like Indian Summers, Sex Education, and Red Rose, she's making her mark on both screens and stages. With her skills and performances, she'll certainly keep impressing fans and critics, paving the way for more fame and wealth in the future.
