Dua Lipa Looks So Sophisticated in a Plunging Backless Minidress

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Dua Lipa just wore an updated version of the classic little black dress.

On Instagram today, the pop star shared photos of herself posing on a rocky shore facing the ocean while wearing an elegant ensemble, courtesy of Celine. The centerpiece of the outfit was a black minidress with a plunging backless silhouette and four pockets attached to the front, studded with ornate gold buttons. For a subtly sophisticated detail, the dress additionally featured white trim embellished with glittering ivory sequins.

Lipa paired the statement piece with caramel-leather gladiator sandals, also by Celine, as well as an array of luxe gold jewelry that included chunky hoop earrings, a delicate wristwatch, and textured rings. The Grammy winner further accessorized with diamond earring cuffs and a bracelet.
