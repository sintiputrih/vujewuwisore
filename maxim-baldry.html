Maxim Baldry (born January 5, 1996, age:26) is an English actor and has been in the acting profession since 2005, and one may recognize him for the role of Stephen in “Mr. Bean’s Holiday,” yeah, the boy who got separated from his father and ended up with Mr. Bean, in the movie that is him.

With time, he has matured and appeared in several more films, including “Lake Placid: Legacy,” “Last Christmas,” “Melodie Saviour,” and a few TV shows, but his most recent appearance in Amazon Prime’s “The Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power” is what he is most proud of. Let’s see how far Maxim has progressed.

Family & Early Lile

Maxim Alexander Baldry, aka Maxim Baldry, was born in London (on 5 January 1996), but he has been all over Moscow, Russia, and Warsaw, Poland; due to his father’s occupation, Simon Baldry worked for Cadbury his work needed a lot of traveling, Maxim along with his mother, Carina Baldry, and elder brother Vic Baldry followed his father around, they finally returned to England in 2003.

Maxim attended a prestigious school in Buckinghamshire, Stoke Mandeville, where he took an interest in sports. Later he learned to dance and sing and attended theater classes at the Jackie Palmer Theater School; these artistic talents came from his grandparents, as his grandmother was a ballerina and his grandfather played jazz professionally; his elder brother took after their father and joined the business of sales and marketing.

Acting Journey

Baldry’s career, like all great actors, began with theater; at a young age, he started performing in his school plays, he sang the musical “Robin Hood,” and began acting in fairy tales like “Aladdin,” “Journey around the World,” and “Peter and the Wolf,” he was so good that representatives of Jackie Palmer’s school became are agents for a while.

He made his baby steps into Hollywood with the film “Mr. Bean’s Holiday,” as Stepan at that time, people might not have known him by name, but he was a familiar face, and his performance was that impactful; he then landed a series in 2007 “Rome,” did a few guest appearances in projects like “Sadie J,” and “Skins.”

It was in 2016 that Maxim was offered a prominent role in a series “Hollyoaks,” then a supporting role in “Lake Placid: Legacy,” for him it was an adventure shooting for this film as it was a bit different from what he was used to doing.

Maxim had a breakthrough in “Years and Years,” which helped him land a role in the film “Last Christmas” as Ed; he was back to series when he was offered “Strike Back” and a guest appearance in “Doctor Who,” he’s also seen in “The Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power,” as Isildur, his might be one of the noteworthy roles of his career.

Baldry is also quite a singer and part of a band called Terra Twin, and he is often on the road along with his friends and band members, performing in various concerts.
