Chris Schumacher Obituary, Hartshorne Ok, Visitation And Funeral Service

Publish date: 2024-02-29

Chris Schumacher Obituary, Death -On the 12th of February, 2023, Christopher B. Schumacher, who was thirty years old and lived in Onalaska, went dead unexpectedly at his residence. Dave and Patty (Poellinger) Schumacher welcomed their son into the world on January 17, 1993 in the city of La Crosse. Chris received his diploma from Logan High School in the year 2011.

He worked as a painter when he was self-employed for a considerable amount of time, and he is currently employed at Performance Food Services. Together with his buddies, he enjoyed going fishing, both on the water and on the ice, as well as playing golf. He was a good-natured individual who was always prepared to assist his family and friends in times of need.

Chris is survived by his mother, Patty Schumacher (Craig Tourville), of Genoa, his brother, Greg Schumacher, of Onalaska, his grandparents, Jerry and Bonnie Poellinger of Eau Claire, his aunts and uncles, Mark and Karin Kiehne of Ettrick, Dan Poellinger, Mary Schumacher, and Maureen Schumacher of La Crosse, Ron Schumacher (Carmen) of Menomonie, Paula Krause (Chris Kettner) of Mazomanie, Steve Schumacher of La Crosse, and many cousins. Chris is also survived by his mother.

Dave Schumacher, his father, his grandparents Ed and Val Schumacher and Dorothy Poellinger, his uncles Jeff, Peter, and Jim Schumacher, and his aunt Kristi Schumacher all passed away before him. Even though his loss has left us with a heavy heart, he is now embarking on a new path. In addition to his father, his grandparents, his uncles, his aunt, and his best friend Dominic, he is joining them.

They will love and care for him in the same way that we ever did. As long as we live, he will remain in our hearts forever. A gathering for family and friends will take place at Schumacher-Kish Funeral & Cremation Services in La Crosse on Friday, February 24, 2023 from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM. The gathering will take place on the day of the funeral.
