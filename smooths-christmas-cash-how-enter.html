Smooth's Christmas Cash: Hear Wham! and win 10,000!

Publish date: 2024-06-26

21 December 2023, 09:44 | Updated: 23 December 2023, 12:59

By Tom Eames

Here at Smooth, in the run-up to Christmas, we’re giving you the chance to win big cash prizes, with Smooth’s Christmas Cash!

Each weekday evening until Friday, December 22, Angie Greaves will be giving away big cash prizes to one lucky winner.

And if you want that to be you, then you need to keep listening for each day's Christmas Cash artist.

We'll reveal each day's prize amount and which artist to look out for every weekday morning during the Breakfast show after 9am.

Today (December 8), your artist is Wham!, and you could win £10,000!

Then, any time you hear us play a song by that particular artist between 10am and 6pm, you need to text the word WIN to 65588. Entrants will be charged £2 plus their standard network rate. You have 10 minutes from the start of the song to enter. If you text after that, your entry won’t count, but may still be charged. You must be 18 or over and live in Great Britain to play this Smooth Network competition.

You can also enter online here.

Plus, you can also enter for free today - find out how on Global Player the official Smooth Radio App.

Please check the full Terms and Conditions for this competition right here.

Good luck!
