Dad who shot to fame rapping the Gruffalo turns attention to The Dinosaur That Pooped books

Publish date: 2024-05-08

A dad who shot to fame for rapping the Guffalo book has turned his attention to more kids books .

Jacob Mitchell has been trying to get his two daughters to learn how to read by rapping the words of famous children's books to them.

Known as MC Grammar, Jacob racked up more than five million views of him rapping the Guffalo to his daughter before he was invited onto the Ellen DeGeneres show in America.

Now he has posted a video of him rapping from two The Dinosaur That Pooped books which were written by McFly stars Dougie Poynter and Tom Fletcher .

In the clip shared on MC Grammar's Twitter he raps from the The Dinosaur That Pooped A Princess and The Dinosaur That Pooped The Bed books.

He took to Twitter to say: "I put this little #RapRead mashup together to celebrate two brilliants books and a terrific trio.

"My girls love ‘The Dinosaur That Pooped...’ series! Sending a big shout out to @tomfletcher, @dougiepoynter and @icandrawdinos.

"Thank you for the smiles, laughter and learning."

Many of his followers took to Twitter to congratulate him on another brilliant video.

@AndieMentor said: "Another masterpiece from the ever inspiring master."

While Boss Woman said: "My 3 year old daughter loved this."

Carla was excited about the video, she said: "Omg amazing. Can't wait to show this to my son in the morning."
